Ice Cream without spoons!

Love ice cream but have spoonaphobia? (spoon-uh-foh-bee-uh n. an intense fear of forgetting to bring a spoon). We have all been there. You go to the store to buy a pint of your favorite flavor of ice cream, you pick a flavor, you go to the register, you pay, and you leave. Gasp! By the time you realized you have forgotten a spoon you are already on a train ready to eat your snack and now you are stuck. That's where we come in. No more crying and wailing on the platform. No more rending of garments. We created ice cream in a can so you can spray it directly into your mouth. So stop your panicking! No more spoons, just on the go blissful eating.

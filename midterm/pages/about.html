What is Spoonless?

Best friend to all ice cream lovers out there, Spoonless is the creative solution to a widespread problem. By bottling wonderful creaminess in a sprayable can, we have eliminated the need to scrounge around for a spoon. We are constantly innovating and developing new flavors and new ways to enjoy ice cream, the most amazing breakfast, dinner dessert, or midnight snack ever invented.
